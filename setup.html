INSTALLATION

Setup & Deployment

Get Ronex configured and running on your MetaTrader 5 terminal in less than 5 minutes.

1

Download from MQL5

Purchase or try the demo of Ronex directly from the official MetaTrader Market tab inside your MetaTrader 5 (MT5) terminal.

2

Include Dashboard File

Make sure the visual dashboard utility file Dashboard.mqh is located in your local terminal's MQL5/Include/ directory.

3

Load onto Chart & Enable Algo

Drag and drop Ronex onto your preferred target chart (e.g., XAUUSD or EURUSD on the M1 or M5 timeframe). Check "Allow Algo Trading" in your MT5 settings.

4

Configure Risk & Run 24/7

Set fixed lot sizes, ADX thresholds, daily boundaries, or load optimized set files. Run on a VPS to ensure uninterrupted continuous execution.
