The Strategy Confluences

Ronex integrates multiple technical filters to prioritize capital safety and capture clean trend expansions.

Trend & Pullback Confluence

Ronex integrates a macro 200 EMA trend filter and a minor 20 EMA pullback baseline, ensuring entries occur strictly at discount prices rather than chasing extended markets.

Engulfing Price Action Trigger

Executes entries with high-probability precision, triggering trades only upon valid bullish or bearish engulfing candlesticks at structural pullback zones.

ADX Momentum Guard

Evaluates the Average Directional Index (ADX) to block entries during choppy, low-liquidity, or ranging conditions, avoiding false breakouts.

Defensive Smart Grid

Features a highly conservative, mathematically calculated recovery grid capped at 3 levels by default, utilizing automatic average break-even basket targets.

Smart Trailing Stop

Secures accumulated profit automatically as trade positions move in your favor. Integrates safety-check levels to prevent broker execution errors.

Daily Equity Protection

Hardcoded Daily Profit Targets and Daily Loss Limits close all active trades and suspend EA operations for the day once hit, preserving your balance.

Fully Customizable Parameters

Fine-tune Ronex directly from the MT5 inputs tab to match your specific risk profile and portfolio structure.

1. Structural Entry Settings

  • EMA 200 Period: Defines the macro trend direction baseline.
  • EMA 20 Period: Defines the minor pullback structure zone.
  • Risk-to-Reward Ratio: Custom targets (e.g., 2.0 = 1:2 R:R).

2. Risk & Account Limits

  • Fixed Lot Size: Sets the base trade entry volume.
  • Max Lot Limit: Caps maximum trade volume for safety.
  • Daily Profit Target Cap ($): Daily yield ceiling to halt operations.
  • Daily Loss Limit Cap ($): Absolute maximum daily loss boundary.

3. Defensive Smart Grid

  • Max Grid Levels: Hard recovery cap (default 3 levels).
  • Grid Spacing (Pips): PIP distance spacing between recovery orders.
  • Grid Multiplier: Multiplier volume (1.0 = same size recovery).
  • Basket Net Target (Pips): Profit target for average break-even exit.

4. Trend Strength Filter (ADX)

  • ADX Period: Average period smoothing for trend tracking.
  • Minimum ADX Level: Entry threshold level (default 22.0).
